BP2 is an app that lets you know the best product and the best price you need.

Why this project?

How many of you want to buy the best product at the best price but do not have enough time to visit several stores and shopping centers. Or just do not want to devote so much effort to make a purchase.

What is the purpose of Best price - best product?

2BP is an app that lets you find the best product at the best price and tells you where you can buy it, based on your tastes and preferences. Products are registered by users or buyers to the system, like the price and description. In order to encourage users to register information about the products, BP2 offers discounts to users that enter more data.
